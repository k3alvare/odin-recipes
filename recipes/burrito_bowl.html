Burrito Bowl

Description

A "burrito bowl" is a dish that deconstructs the typical ingredients of a burrito into a bowl that is eaten usually with a fork. It reduces the mess that one may experience when eating a burrito. It also allows the eater to choose the perfect bite, as they can hand-pick what they want in a bite!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Lime
  • Salsa
  • Salt
  • Corn
  • Beans (Black or Pinto)
  • Cilantro
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Season chicken with olive oil, salt and lime juice and allow it to marinate for a couple hours
  2. Cook chicken on a skillet until cooked thoroughly
  3. In another pot cook the rice, and add lime and cilantro
  4. Layer rice first into bowl, then add the beans, salsa, corn, romaine lettuce, chicken
  5. Enjoy!
