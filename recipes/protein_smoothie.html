Protein Smoothie

Description

A protein smoothie is compromised of a variety ingredients typically of fruits, oats, liquid (water, milk, non-dairy milk), and protein powder. Protein smoothies are a good drink to prepare especially after a workout as they are nutritious and refreshing!

Ingredients

  • Liquid (milk, non-dairy milk, or water)
  • Fruit (blueberries, strawberries, mango, peach, bananas, i.e. any fruit!)
  • Protein powder of your choice
  • Ice
  • Chia seeds

Steps

  1. Pour some of your choice of liquid into a blender (just a tiny bit)
  2. Add a scoop of protein powder into the blender
  3. Evenly distribute and add the rest of the ingredients
  4. Blend all the ingredients together until smoothie-like consistency!
  5. Enjoy your smoothie!
