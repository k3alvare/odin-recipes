Quesadilla

Description

A quesdilla is an easy snack to make! However, by adding more ingredients it could easily be taken up a notch and eaten as a gourmet meal!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla (Corn or Flour)
  • Cheese (Mexican Blend)
    • Optional Ingredients:
  • Sour Cream
  • Salsa
  • Shredded Chicken

Steps

  1. Warm up tortilla on skillet on medium-high heat
  2. Once slightly warmed up for a couple of minutes, add as much cheese as your heart desires
    3. Optional Step
  3. Add shredded chicken on top of tortilla and cheese
  4. Fold quesadilla in half and cook until flour is golden brown
  5. Use salsa/sour cream as dipping for your quesadilla and enjoy
